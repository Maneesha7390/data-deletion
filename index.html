Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data from our app, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to maneesha@ahex.co.in with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your Facebook user ID and any relevant information in the email to help us identify your data.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and notify you once your data has been deleted.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team at maneesha@ahex.co.in.
